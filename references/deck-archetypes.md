# Clash Royale Deck Archetypes

Common war deck archetypes and their synergy patterns.

## Lumberloon (Lumberjack + Balloon)
- **Core**: Lumberjack + Balloon
- **Synergy**: Lumberjack dies in front of Balloon, dropping Rage
- **Support**: Splash damage (Valkyrie, Baby Dragon), cheap spells (Arrows, The Log)
- **Defense**: Building (Tombstone, Cannon), air troops (Bats, Mega Minion)
- **Avg Elixir**: 3.0-3.5

## Spell Bait (Log Bait / Goblin Bait)
- **Core**: Multiple spell-bait cards (Goblin Gang, Goblins, Dart Goblin, Goblin Barrel)
- **Synergy**: Opponent can't spell all bait cards, something always connects
- **Support**: Mini tank (Mini P.E.K.K.A, Knight), medium spell (Fireball, Rocket)
- **Defense**: Inferno Tower or Goblin Cage, cheap spell (Zap, The Log)
- **Avg Elixir**: 2.9-3.3

## P.E.K.K.A Bridge Spam
- **Core**: P.E.K.K.A + bridge spam units (Bandit, Dark Prince, Royal Ghost)
- **Synergy**: P.E.K.K.A defends then counter-pushes, bridge spam in opposite lane
- **Support**: Magic Archer for piercing value, Electro Wizard for stun
- **Defense**: P.E.K.K.A is the defense, building optional (Cannon)
- **Avg Elixir**: 3.8-4.2

## Royal Hogs Split Lane
- **Core**: Royal Hogs + fireball bait elements
- **Synergy**: Split Royal Hogs both lanes, opponent can't defend both
- **Support**: Buildings (Furnace, Tesla), ranged DPS (Musketeer, Flying Machine)
- **Defense**: Knight/Valkyrie as mini-tank, cheap spell (Zap, Arrows)
- **Avg Elixir**: 3.3-3.8

## Hog Cycle
- **Core**: Hog Rider + cheap cycle cards
- **Synergy**: Fast cycle back to Hog Rider, constant pressure
- **Support**: Musketeer/Archers for DPS, Ice Spirit/Skeletons for cycle
- **Defense**: Cannon/Tesla, Fireball/Earthquake
- **Avg Elixir**: 2.6-3.0

## Mega Knight Control
- **Core**: Mega Knight for splash defense + counter-push
- **Synergy**: Drop Mega Knight on enemy pushes, then support behind
- **Support**: Inferno Dragon/Mini P.E.K.K.A for tanks, Bats/Minions for air
- **Defense**: Mega Knight IS the defense
- **Avg Elixir**: 3.5-4.0

## Golem Beatdown
- **Core**: Golem + support troops behind
- **Synergy**: Build massive push behind Golem, overwhelm opponent
- **Support**: Night Witch, Lumberjack, Baby Dragon behind Golem
- **Defense**: Minimal - rely on counter-push after Golem push
- **Avg Elixir**: 4.0-4.5

## Graveyard Control
- **Core**: Graveyard + tank (Knight, Ice Golem, Valkyrie)
- **Synergy**: Tank at tower + Graveyard = skeletons chip tower
- **Support**: Poison to protect Graveyard, splash for defense
- **Defense**: Buildings, Tornado for king tower activation
- **Avg Elixir**: 3.2-3.8

## War Deck Building Rules

### No Card Overlap
Each card can only appear in ONE of the 4 war decks.

### Evo/Hero Slot Rules
- 1 Hero slot (dedicated)
- 1 Evo slot (dedicated)
- 1 Flex slot (Hero OR Evo)
- Total: 3 enabled evo/hero cards per deck
- More evo/hero cards can be in the deck but won't be "active"

### Priority Order
1. Synergy: Build a coherent archetype first
2. Card Level: Higher level cards preferred
3. Heroes: Use hero-unlocked cards in hero/flex slots
4. Evos: Use evo-unlocked cards in evo/flex slots

## Best Deck Building Rules

### Cards May Repeat
Unlike war decks, the same card CAN appear in multiple best decks. This allows
building the strongest possible deck for each archetype without worrying about
card availability across decks.

### Different Archetypes Required
Each of the 4 best decks MUST use a different archetype (e.g., Beatdown, Bait,
Bridge Spam, Cycle). The goal is variety in playstyle, not card uniqueness.

### Evo/Hero Slot Rules (same as war decks)
- 1 Hero slot (dedicated)
- 1 Evo slot (dedicated)
- 1 Flex slot (Hero OR Evo)
- Total: 3 enabled evo/hero cards per deck

### Priority Order (same as war decks)
1. Synergy: Build a coherent archetype first
2. Card Level: Higher level cards preferred
3. Heroes: Use hero-unlocked cards in hero/flex slots
4. Evos: Use evo-unlocked cards in evo/flex slots
